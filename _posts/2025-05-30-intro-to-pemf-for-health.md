**Pulsed Electromagnetic Field therapy (PEMF)** is one of the most promising, low-risk technologies for improving cellular health and promoting recovery — and it’s more accessible than you might think.

---

## ⚡ What Is PEMF?

PEMF uses low-frequency electromagnetic pulses to stimulate cells. The goal is to *restore natural energy and function* at the cellular level, especially where injury, fatigue, or dysfunction is present.

Think of it as "charging your cells’ batteries" — helping them return to a healthy state.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

PEMF influences:

- **Ion channels** in the cell membrane
- **Mitochondrial energy production (ATP)**
- **Blood circulation and oxygenation**
- **Inflammation regulation**

It’s non-invasive and can be used regularly with few side effects.

---

## 🏠 At-Home Use

There are consumer PEMF mats and devices available today. While clinical-grade systems are expensive, **entry-level mats** and **wearable rings or coils** can be found for a few hundred dollars.

Use cases include:

- Sleep optimization
- Recovery from workouts or injury
- Pain management
- Mood support

---

## ⚠️ What to Look For

When exploring PEMF devices:

- ✅ Frequency range: Look for 1–50 Hz (often cited in studies)
- ✅ Intensity: Lower intensities (µT–mT range) are generally safer for home use
- ✅ Certification: Look for FDA-registered or CE-certified devices

- **PubMed (2020s):** Ongoing studies show efficacy for pain, bone healing, and depression
